Buzzwords to Go

What is this?

Buzzwords to Go is a tongue-in-cheek mobile web app that serves up three random business buzzwords with every shake of your phone. Whether you're preparing for a pitch deck, brainstorming your next "innovative solution," or just need a dose of corporate humor, we've got you covered with the finest selection of synergistic terminology.

How does it work?

Just shake your phone (or tap/click anywhere on the buzzwords) and watch as three fresh buzzwords materialize before your eyes. It's perfect for:

  • Breaking the ice at startup meetups
  • Generating "innovative" product names
  • Creating impressively vague mission statements
  • Having a laugh at corporate speak
  • Impressing your cat with your business acumen

Why did we build this?

In the spirit of leveraging cross-platform synergies to facilitate paradigm-shifting ideation frameworks... we thought it would be fun. This project serves as a lightweight example of modern web development with responsive design, device motion APIs, and a healthy dose of humor about the business world's love affair with buzzwords.

Built with vanilla JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, Buzzwords to Go proves you don't need a framework to create engaging mobile experiencesâ€”just a good idea and some well-chosen words.

Tech Stack

This app is built with modern web technologies and best practices:

  • Vanilla JavaScript - No frameworks, just pure JS
  • Vite - Fast build tooling with HMR
  • PostCSS + Autoprefixer - Browser compatibility
  • ESLint + Prettier - Code quality and consistency
  • Image optimization - Automatic compression
  • Light/Dark mode - Respect user preferences
  • Device Motion API - Shake to refresh on mobile

Open Source

Buzzwords to Go is open source and available on GitHub. Feel free to contribute, fork, or use it as a learning resource. We welcome pull requests, bug reports, and suggestions for new buzzwords to add to our collection.
